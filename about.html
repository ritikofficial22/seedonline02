About us



What We Are?

SEED (Support for Employability and Entrepreneur Development) is a program working for government and non-government firms on multiple projects. We take projects from companies and hire employees on our pay scale to complete the projects. We support Entrepreneurship Development Program in which we also provide employees to the startups.


Our Purpose

At SEED, our purpose is to provide multiple services to the companies by completing their projects on time. We are giving employment to the people on our end and completing govt. and non-govt. projects by taking the tender of work. We have direct contacts with Companies which are working on project basis and hire employees only for the project period, whereas we take projects from them and hire employees for a permanent period on our pay scale.


Our Vision

We are going to open incubation centres in every district in India where we will support the Entrepreneurship Development Program. New Entrepreneurs will be guided by us for fund raising and we will also provide funds to them on our end. This way new entrepreneurs will start businesses in which the employment of the people will be done from our end. We take bids from companies on different projects and employ people on our end and our pay scale. This way the project work will be done for companies and the unemployed people will also get the jobs.
